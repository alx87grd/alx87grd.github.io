Robotized Transmission for Electric Vehicles

Abstract

During the last two year of my bachelor’s degree, I was part of Project Phoebus, consisting in the development of a high performance electric car, based on a VW Golf. I developed a two-speed robotized transmission to be used with a TM4 electric motor.

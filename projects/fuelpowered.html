Fuel-Powered Fluidic Muscle

Abstract

During my research in CAMUS laboratory, I analyzed the feasibility of using combustion in an air-muscle (lean burn of hydrogen). I conducted full cycle efficiency analysis and designed, manufactured and built a test bench.

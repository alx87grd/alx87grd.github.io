Modeling

Most robot arm control schemes use a kinematic and/or a dynamic model of the arm. Here this section presents an overview of the different types of model that controllers will leverage and the nomenclature.

Kinematics

Forward kinematic: \begin{align} r = f( q ) \end{align}

Differential kinematic

\begin{align} \dot{r} = J(q) \dot{q} \quad \text{with} \quad J(q) = \frac{\partial f}{\partial q} \end{align}

Dynamics

\begin{align} H(q) \ddot{q} + C(q,\dot{q})\dot{q} + d(q,\dot{q}) + g(q) = B(q) \tau + J(q)^T f_e \end{align}

An Elite Research Environment

Students in our lab benefit from a unique multidisciplinary ecosystem in Sherbrooke, blending academic excellence with high-impact industrial collaboration.

3IT – Institute for Interdisciplinary Innovation

A world-class institute where researchers from engineering, science, and medicine collaborate. Features high-end mechanical workshops and instrumented test rooms for robotics.

Learn more →

Createk – Design & Prototyping Group

A dynamic cluster of professors and professionals dedicated to developing advanced technologies in robotics, actuators, and vehicles with a strong "Maker" culture.

Learn more →

CTA – Centre de technologies avancées

A unique partnership between BRP and Université de Sherbrooke, focused on developing the next generation of motorized vehicles and sustainable mobility solutions.

Life in Sherbrooke

Université de Sherbrooke offers an unparalleled quality of life, balancing world-class research facilities with the beauty of mountains, forests, and lakes. Sherbrooke is a vibrant, affordable student city located about 1.5 hours from Montreal.

  • Excellent research institutes (3IT, CTA)
  • Dynamic "Maker" community
  • High-quality of life / Quick Nature access

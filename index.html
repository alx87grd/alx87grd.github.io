About

Greetings! I am Alexandre Girard a roboticist and Professor at Université de Sherbrooke, Qc, Canada. I am especially interested in the science of controlling how things move, from mechanical design up to control laws and algorithms: robots, mechanisms, actuators, sensors, vehicles, etc. Feel free to contact me if your are interested in robotics research projects!

If you are a student looking for a thesis project and/or position in my laboratory: see this page. If you are looking for a university partner to develop new technologies and/or solve hard motion control problems, please contact me.
